Our story

From night markets to your neighborhood

Taikyfood began as a pop-up in the bustling streets of Ho Chi Minh City. Today, our team of chefs brings authentic recipes and modern culinary artistry together in a kitchen built for delivery. Every sauce is slow-brewed, every dumpling folded by hand, and every delivery packed with care.
